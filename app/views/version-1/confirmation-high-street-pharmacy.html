What to do next

Contact your selected pharmacy today or tomorrow for your consultation.

You can either:

  • go to the pharmacy in person
  • call the pharmacy

You need to tell them you were referred by 111 online.

If you call the pharmacy

You can ask for phone consultation if you would prefer one.

You may still need to go to the pharmacy in person if:

  • the pharmacy is too busy to answer the phone
  • you have a problem the pharmacist would like to see in person, like a rash
