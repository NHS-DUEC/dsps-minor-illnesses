What to do next

Contact the pharmacy today or tomorrow for your consultation.

When you call them, they will describe how the consultation will take place, which will usually be over video. You can ask for a phone consultation if you prefer.

You need to tell them you were referred by 111 online.

If they need to see you in person to assess the problem they will explain how that will happen.

This might be:

  • visiting their local site to have a consultation in a private room
  • being referred on to another local pharmacy in your area
