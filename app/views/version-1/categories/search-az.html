All topics (116)

Topics beginning with A

  1. Accidentally taking something harmful

    Accidentally taking, swallowing, breathing in or injecting something poisonous or harmful. Taking too much of any medicine by mistake.

  2. Ankle or foot injury

    An injury to the ankle or foot in the last 7 days that does not have any type of cut or wound. Sprained ankle.

  3. Ankle or foot injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to the ankle or foot in the last 7 days that has any type of cut or wound.

  4. Any problem affecting the skin

    Includes rashes, lumps, spots, itchy skin, moles and allergic reactions.

  5. Arm or shoulder injury

    An injury to the arm or shoulder within the last 7 days, that has not cut the skin. A dislocated shoulder or arm.

  6. Arm or shoulder injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to the arm or shoulder in the last 7 days, that has caused a cut or wound.

Topics beginning with B

  1. Behaving strangely

    Any odd behaviour. Seeing or hearing things that aren't there. Cannot stay awake. New changes or an existing condition getting worse.

  2. Bites or stings

    A bite or sting from animals, humans, insects, birds or reptiles. Bitten by a dog, cat, pet, wild animal, spider, tick or bugs. Stung by a wasp, bee, mosquito, horsefly or stinging plants and nettles. Swollen, itchy, red, irritated or burning skin and allergic reactions.

  3. Bleeding from the bottom

    Spots of blood on the toilet paper. Streaks of blood in poo. Red or black coloured poo.

  4. Bleeding from the vagina

    Heavy periods, bleeding after sex, unexpected bleeding, or any bleeding after the menopause. Bleeding or spotting when pregnant.

  5. Blisters

    Blisters from something rubbing against the skin, such as new shoes.

  6. Blocked or stuffy nose

    A congested or blocked nose. Sinus problems.

  7. Blood pressure problems

    Worries about blood pressure being too high or too low.

  8. Bottom pain, swelling lump or itch

    Pain, itching or swelling in or around the bum (anus or rectum). Lumps in or around the bottom.

  9. Breast and nipple problems

    Any problems with the breast or nipple. Lumps, pain or any worries about skin changes.

  10. Breastfeeding problems

    Problems getting the baby to attach to the breast. Cracked or sore nipples, or a painful breast that is making breastfeeding difficult.

  11. Breathing problems

    Checks for things like: asthma, chest infection, pneumonia, bronchitis, COVID-19

    Wheezing, panting or feeling out of breath. Difficult or noisy breathing.

  12. Burns from heat

    A scald from hot liquid or a burn from touching something hot, such as an iron or a flame.

Topics beginning with C

  1. Cannot feel baby move as much

    Worries that the baby isn't moving as much as usual. Baby's movements are slower or have stopped.

  2. Catheter problems

    A problem with a blocked or leaking catheter. Pain from a catheter. Trouble removing or inserting a catheter.

  3. Chemical burns

    Burns caused by chemicals.

  4. Chest or upper back injury

    An injury to the chest or upper back within the last 7 days, that has not cut the skin. Broken, painful or bruised ribs. Injuries from the collarbone to bottom of the ribcage.

  5. Chest or upper back injury with a cut or wound

    A wound on the chest or upper back (the collarbone to the bottom of the ribcage) within the last 7 days. Broken, painful or bruised ribs with a wound.

  6. Chest pain

    Checks for things like: heartburn, angina

    Any chest or upper back pain. Chest pain with or without arm, shoulder, neck or jaw pain. Tightness in the chest.

  7. Cold arm or arm changing colour

    A cold, painless arm. Colour changes include skin being lighter or darker than normal in one or both arms.

  8. Cold leg or leg changing colour

    A cold, painless leg. Colour changes include skin being lighter or darker than normal in one or both legs.

  9. Cold or flu

    A running nose, sneezing, aching muscles or headache. Fever (high temperature), coughing, sore throat, or pain in the face.

  10. Constipation

    Cannot poo or finding it hard to poo, with or without tummy pain.

  11. Cough

    Checks for things like: chest infection, hay fever, COVID-19

    Hacking, tickly or dry cough, without other cold symptoms. Coughing up phlegm or mucus.

  12. Coughing or vomiting up blood

    Coughing up blood in sick (vomit), phlegm or mucus.

  13. Cuts, scratches or grazes

    A small cut, scratch or graze that might have gravel, dirt or grit inside.

Topics beginning with D

  1. Dental injuries

    Dental problems caused by an injury, such as knocked out or broken teeth. Injuries to the gums.

  2. Diabetic blood sugar problems

    Diabetic blood sugar levels are too high or too low. A sudden problem with blood sugar levels.

  3. Diarrhoea

    Runny or watery poo, with or without pain in the tummy.

  4. Diarrhoea and vomiting

    Runny or watery poo and being sick, with or without tummy pain.

  5. Difficulty swallowing

    Having trouble swallowing or unable to swallow, without a sore throat.

  6. Discharge from the vagina

    Vaginal discharge, with or without tummy pain.

  7. Dizziness

    Feeling dizzy, off balance, lightheaded or as if the room is spinning (vertigo).

  8. Drink and drug problems

    Alcohol or substance misuse. Seeing or hearing things that aren't there. Trouble sleeping.

  9. Drunk too much alcohol

    Too much to drink. Being sick or feeling ill because of alcoholic drinks.

Topics beginning with E

  1. Earache

    Ear pain, swelling, or fluid leaking from the ear. A painful blocked ear. New deafness with pain. Ringing in the ear.

  2. Eye injury

    An injury to the eye or eye socket within the last 7 days without any cut or wound.

  3. Eye injury with a cut

    An injury to the eye or eye socket within the last 7 days that has caused a cut or wound.

  4. Eye or eyelid problems

    Sore, yellow, red or bloodshot eyes. Loss or changes in vision. Floaters or flashes. Blurred vision. Swollen, sticky, itchy or sore eyelids. A lump on the eyelid.

Topics beginning with F

  1. Falling, fainting or passing out

    A fall, faint, blackout or loss of consciousness, with no injury.

  2. Finger or thumb injury

    A finger or thumb injury within the last 7 days that has not cut the skin.

  3. Finger or thumb injury with a cut or wound

    A finger or thumb injury within the last 7 days that has cut the skin.

  4. Fingernail injury

    Any injury to the fingernail or nails in the last 7 days.

  5. Fluid or wax coming from the ear with no loss of hearing

    Fluid, ear wax or watery discharge from one or both ears but without any loss of hearing.

Topics beginning with G

  1. Genital injury

    An injury anywhere on the genitals in the last 7 days that has not cut the skin.

  2. Genital injury with cut or wound

    An injury to the genitals within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Cuts, wounds or grazes anywhere on the vagina or the area between the anus and vagina.

  3. Glue on the skin

    Something is glued to the skin, or the skin has been glued together.

  4. Groin pain or swelling

    Pain or swelling in the groin (where the top of your leg meets the front of your body).

Topics beginning with H

  1. Hair falling out or thinning

    Hair loss from any part of the body. Losing hair in patches or clumps.

  2. Hand or wrist injury

    A hand or wrist injury in the last 7 days that has not cut the skin.

  3. Hand or wrist injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to the hand or wrist within the last 7 days that has cut the skin.

  4. Head, face or neck injury

    An injury to the head, face or neck within the last 7 days that didn't cut the skin.

  5. Head, face or neck injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to any part of the face (but not eyes or teeth), head or neck, which has cut the skin, within the last 7 days.

  6. Headache or migraine

    Throbbing or intense pain or a tight band around the head. Visual problems like flashing lights with a headache.

  7. Hearing problems or blocked ear

    Hearing problems or deafness without earache. Blocked ear or fluid leaking from the ear with no pain.

  8. Heatstroke

    Overheating caused by being in hot temperatures or by exercising too much.

  9. Hiccups

    Hiccups, with possible fever. Hiccups won't stop.

  10. High temperature with no other symptoms

    Chills, shivering or hot to touch with no other problems.

Topics beginning with I

  1. Itchy vagina

    Sore or itchy vagina.

Topics beginning with L

  1. Late or missed period

    Late or missed periods. No periods. Periods haven't started. Periods have stopped.

  2. Leg injury

    A leg injury to the hip, thigh, knee, calf, shin or ankle within the last 7 days that didn't cut the skin.

  3. Leg injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to the leg, hip, thigh, knee, calf, shin or ankle in the last 7 days, that has a cut, wound or graze.

  4. Locked or aching jaw

    Problems opening or closing the mouth or jaw.

  5. Loss of bowel control

    An urge to poo that cannot be controlled. Soiling or staining underwear. May also include constipation or diarrhoea, bloating or wind.

  6. Loss or change to your sense of smell or taste

    You cannot smell or taste anything, or things smell or taste different to normal.

  7. Lower back injury

    An injury to the lower back (below the rib cage) or bottom in the last 7 days that has not cut the skin.

  8. Lower back injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to the lower back (below the rib cage) or bottom in the last 7 days with a cut, wound or graze.

  9. Lower back pain

    Pain or ache in the lower part of the back.

Topics beginning with M

  1. Menopause

    Menopause, perimenopause symptoms. Hormone replacement therapy (HRT), other treatments.

  2. Mouth ulcers

    Mouth, lip, tongue or gum ulcers. Sore white or grey patches inside the mouth.

Topics beginning with N

  1. Nosebleed

    A bleeding nose.

  2. Nosebleed after an injury

    A bleeding nose following an injury.

Topics beginning with O

  1. Older wound problems

    A problem with a wound that was treated by a doctor or nurse more than 7 days ago.

Topics beginning with P

  1. Pain in the side of the body

    Pain on either side of the body, between the ribs and hips. Affecting one side only at the back or with stomach pain.

  2. Palpitations

    Feeling the heart is beating too fast or too slow. Pounding or irregular heartbeat. Racing heart, fluttering in the chest, skipped beats.

  3. Pre-menstrual syndrome (PMS)

    Feeling unwell before a period.

  4. Problems peeing

    Pain, stinging or burning when peeing. Only being able to pee very small amounts or cannot pee at all. Bloody, red or pink pee. Peeing too much or smelly pee.

  5. Problems with drains, stitches, dressings, plaster casts or metal rods

    New pain or problems with dressings, stitches, staples, plaster casts, metal rods, drainage tubes or central line catheters that were inserted less than 7 days ago.

Topics beginning with S

  1. Sexual health problems

    A problem with contraception or sexual health. Worries about sexually transmitted infections (STIs). HIV/AIDS.

  2. Shoulder problems

    Ache or stiffness in one or both shoulders. Unusual feelings or weakness.

  3. Sinusitis

    A problem with the sinuses. Face pain around the cheeks, eyes or forehead. A runny nose and a high temperature.

  4. Sleep problems

    Problems sleeping, getting to sleep or staying asleep. Tiredness. Insomnia.

  5. Something in the ear

    An object stuck inside the ear. An insect in the ear.

  6. Something in the eye

    Dirt, dust or an object has gone into the eyes. Something sprayed or splashed into the eyes.

  7. Something stuck in the bottom

    An object stuck in the back passage.

  8. Something stuck in the nose

    An object stuck inside a nostril.

  9. Something stuck in the vagina

    An item such as a tampon, cap, condom or other object, that's got stuck in the vagina.

  10. Sore throat

    A painful throat, with or without problems swallowing. A hoarse, croaky or lost voice, with or without a sore throat.

  11. Splinters or problems with piercings

    A problem with a small object stuck in the skin. Wood or glass splinters. Problems with piercings.

  12. Stoma problems

    A problem with any stoma or bag attached to the body.

  13. Stomach ache or pain

    Checks for things like: indigestion, heartburn, food poisoning, stomach ulcer, gallstones, kidney stones

    Tummy pain with or without symptoms such as being sick, diarrhoea or a high temperature. Trapped wind. Abdominal pain.

  14. Stomach or side injury

    An injury to the tummy, stomach or sides within the last 7 days that has not cut the skin. Stomach injury, flank injury.

  15. Stomach or side injury with a cut or wound

    A wound to the tummy, stomach or side within the last 7 days. Flank injury. Stomach injury.

  16. Stress, depression or anxiety

    Low mood, feeling depressed or stressed. Panic attacks or anxiety. Trouble sleeping. Seeing or hearing things that aren't there. Drink and drug problems.

  17. Sunburn

    Burns caused by the sun or a tanning sunbed or lamp. Red, warm, sore, tender, or peeling skin.

  18. Swallowed or breathed in an object

    Accidentally swallowing or inhaling something. Choking or inhaling an object. Something stuck in the throat.

  19. Swallowed or used something to harm yourself

    Swallowing, breathing in or injecting something to harm yourself on purpose.

  20. Swollen or painful arm

    Pain or swelling in the arm or elbow.

  21. Swollen or painful face or neck

    Swelling, lumps or pain in the face or neck. Swollen glands.

  22. Swollen or painful hand, finger or wrist

    Pain or swelling in the hand, fingers, nails or wrist.

  23. Swollen or painful legs, knees, ankles or feet

    Checks for things like: sciatica, gout, ingrown toenail, cramp, ulcers

    Painful or swollen legs, thighs, knees, ankles or feet. Pain or swelling in the hips or bum. Heel pain.

Topics beginning with T

  1. Tiredness

    Feeling tired or exhausted (fatigue) most of the time, or tiring easily.

  2. Toe injury

    A toe injury within the last 7 days that has not cut the skin.

  3. Toe injury with a cut or wound

    An injury to one or more toes within the last 7 days that has cut the skin.

  4. Toenail injury

    An injury to one or more toenails within the last 7 days. Painful, broken, ingrown or loose toenails.

  5. Toothache after an injury

    Toothache and pain caused by an injury, such as broken, cracked or knocked out teeth. Bleeding or injuries to the gums or mouth. Swelling or lumps on the face or jaw. Problems opening or closing the mouth.

  6. Toothache, swelling and other dental problems

    Bleeding from the mouth, teeth or gums. Painful teeth or gums. Swelling or lumps on the gums, mouth or jaw. A crown, bridge, fillings, denture or brace problem. A broken tooth, or teeth not caused by an injury.

  7. Trembling or shaking

    Shaking or trembling (tremor).

Topics beginning with U

  1. Unusual skin or body sensations

    Strange feelings in the skin anywhere on the body. Numbness, burning, tingling or pins and needles.

Topics beginning with V

  1. Vaginal lump or swelling

    Any lump, cyst, swelling or pain in or around the vagina. Swollen or sore labia.

  2. Vomiting or nausea

    Being or feeling sick.
