Skin, hair and nails includes rashes, moles, bites and stings, small cuts and grazes

Skin

  1. Any problem affecting the skin

    Includes bites and stings, moles, rashes and allergies

  2. Bites or stings

    A bite or sting from animals, humans, insects, birds or reptiles. Swollen, itchy, red, reaction. Bitten by a dog, cat, a pet or wild animal. Stung by a wasp, bee, mosquito or horsefly. Spider, tick or bites from bugs. Burning or stinging pain. Irritation. Stinging plants and nettles. Lump, swelling or blister.

  3. Blisters

    Blisters from something rubbing against the skin, such as new shoes.

  4. Cuts, scratches or grazes

    A small cut, scratch or graze that might have gravel, dirt or grit inside.

  5. Fingernail injury

    Any injury to the fingernail or nails in the last 7 days.

  6. Glue on the skin

    Something is glued to the skin, or the skin has been glued together.

  7. Hair falling out or thinning

    Hair loss from any part of the body. Losing hair in patches or clumps.

  8. Splinters or problems with piercings

    A problem with a small object stuck in the skin. Wood or glass splinters. Problems with piercings.

  9. Sunburn

    Burns caused by the sun or a tanning sunbed or lamp. Red, warm, sore, tender, or peeling skin.

  10. Toenail injury

    An injury to one or more toenails within the last 7 days. Painful, broken, ingrown or loose toenails.

  11. Unusual skin or body sensations

    Strange feelings in the skin anywhere on the body. Numbness, burning, tingling or pins and needles.
