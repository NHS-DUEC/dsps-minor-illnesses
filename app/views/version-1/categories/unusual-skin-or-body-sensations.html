Unusual skin or body sensations

  1. Any problem affecting the skin

    Includes bites and stings, moles, rashes and allergies

  2. Breast and nipple problems

    Any problems with the breast or nipple. Lumps, pain or any worries about skin changes.

  3. Cold arm or arm changing colour

    A cold, painless arm. Dead arm. White, blue, or black colour in one or both arms.

  4. Cold leg or leg changing colour

    A cold, painless leg. Dead leg. White, blue or black colour in one or both legs.

  5. Hiccups

    Hiccups, with possible fever. Hiccups won't stop.

  6. Loss or change to your sense of smell or taste

    You cannot smell or taste anything, or things smell or taste different to normal.

  7. Unusual skin or body sensations

    Strange feelings in the skin anywhere on the body. Numbness, burning, tingling or pins and needles.
