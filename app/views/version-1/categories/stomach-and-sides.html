Stomach and sides

  1. Diarrhoea and vomiting

    Runny or watery poo and being sick, with or without tummy pain.

  2. Pain in the side of the body

    Pain on either side of the body, between the ribs and hips. Affecting one side only at the back (love handles) or with stomach pain.

  3. Stomach ache or pain Checks for things like: indigestion, heartburn, food poisoning, stomach ulcer, gallstones, kidney stones

    Tummy pain with or without symptoms such as being sick, diarrhoea or a high temperature. Trapped wind. Abdominal pain. Swollen stomach.

  4. Stomach or side injury

    An injury to the tummy, stomach or sides within the last 7 days that hasn't cut the skin. Stomach injury, flank injury.

  5. Stomach or side injury with a cut or wound

    A wound to the tummy, stomach or side within the last 7 days. Flank injury. Stomach injury.

  6. Vomiting or nausea

    Being or feeling sick.
