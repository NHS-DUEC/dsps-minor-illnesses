Pharmacist consultation service

A pharmacist can help with your symptoms. You can refer yourself to a pharmacist for a one-to-one consultation. You can choose a pharmacist to visit, or have a video or phone consultation.

This service needs some more details from you to find your NHS number.

Pharmacists are trained to deal with minor illnesses and common conditions.

You can get advice, treatment or referral to another NHS service if needed.

If appropriate, pharmacists can prescribe antibiotics or antivirals for a small number of common conditions.

We will send your completed referral to the pharmacist so they are prepared.

When you contact them the pharmacist will explain what will happen next.
